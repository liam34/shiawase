Shiawase Ramen

At Shiawase Ramen, we serve handcrafted Japanese ramen made with rich, slow-simmered broths, fresh noodles, and quality ingredients. From classic tonkatsu to spicy miso and vegetarian options, every bowl is packed with authentic flavor. Visit us in Edmonton for a cozy dine-in experience or order online for takeout.

Excellent little ramen spot for the area. I tried the tonkotsu and it’s the most opaque and creamy broth of all the places I’ve tried in Edmonton. The broth was the star. Egg was slightly overcooked for my personal liking, yolk not runny but nice jelly-like consistency. Pork was excellent, wonderful char. The noodles were different, thinner and reminded me more of wheat noodles, didn’t the same bounce and chewiness I usually expect and appreciate - but I like how it’s different, I could see it growing on me. Service was good overall, the server was very genuine and friendly. Very chill and relaxed atmosphere. Clean and minimalistic. Would definitely go back if I’m in the area.

We were looking for a new ramen place and Shiwase came up with positive reviews. I gotta say, this place is amazing! The first time we went we had the mapo tofu ramen and the black garlic tonkotsu ramen. They were both so delicious. The broth was very flavourful and the portions were just right. The chasu was really tender and delicious. I highly recommend. Especially the Black Garlic one if you don’t know what to pick, or it’s your first time having ramen. BUT, the best dish that changed my life was their Chicken Nanban. It’s rare to see nanbans so we ordered it and it was legit life changing. The chicken was really crisp and light and the sauce? It was just so delicious too. I think it’s just eggs and mayo? I couldn’t stop thinking about it for weeks. We also got the bao buns and they were really good too! They hit just the right spot with a good balance of salty and sour with the pickled side and cucumbers. Our second trip I just wanted the Nanbandon and my bf got the Oyakodon. They were extremely filling but delicious. We even ordered another Nanban on the side. So I highly recommend coming to this place! And get the Nanban you won’t regret it. Plus the prices are really fair and it’s family owned. We will definitely come back again.

- Cherry Santos
