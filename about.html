Shiawase Ramen

Welcome to Shiawase Ramen – Edmonton's True Taste of Japan At Shiawase Ramen, we bring the rich, soulful flavors of traditional Japanese ramen straight to the heart of Edmonton. Every bowl is crafted with care, using slow-simmered broths, fresh noodles, and premium ingredients to deliver an unforgettable dining experience that’s both comforting and crave-worthy.Whether you’re a ramen enthusiast or new to the world of Japanese cuisine, you’ll find something to love on our menu—from our signature Tonkatsu pork ramen, to hearty miso broths, spicy selections, and vegetarian-friendly options. We also serve a curated selection of Japanese sides, appetizers, and drinks to complete your meal.Our cozy, modern space offers a warm welcome to friends, families, and solo diners alike. Dine in, take out, or order online—however you choose to enjoy it, Shiawase Ramen is your go-to spot for genuine Japanese ramen in Edmonton.
